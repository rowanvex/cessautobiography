The start

I was born in Antipolo City on December 15, 2004. My mother is a housewife, and my father is a business manager. I come from a broken family because we no longer live in the same house. One childhood memory I will never forget is when I accidentally swallowed a five-peso coin. I was rushed to the hospital because I was so stubborn, but I survived — thankfully, they managed to avoid surgery, and I fully recovered.

Experiences

My school life right now is not easy because I am a working student. It’s difficult to balance my studies and my job, and sometimes I struggle to keep up due to my tight schedule and lack of time. Still, I manage to push through and do what I have to do. Even though it’s hard, I continue to fight for my dreams because I have no one else to rely on but myself.

Yes, there are times when I feel sad and question why I ended up in this situation, but I remain thankful because I’m able to handle it. I know that the Lord is always with me — giving me strength to face every challenge now and in the future — because I believe that no problem is impossible to overcome.

Memories

I’ve learned a lot from life and from every challenge I’ve faced. Even when I fell, I stood up again for my dreams. I’m determined to finish my studies, build a stable and successful life, and repay my parents’ love and sacrifices. With faith in the Lord, I believe I can overcome any hardship and become a better person, ready to achieve my dreams through my own effort and perseverance.

Realizations

The only thing I can say now is that I am truly proud of myself. I’ve become stronger and braver, able to face all the challenges that come my way.

I can now provide for my family and support my studies financially, and I’ve learned to stand on my own without expecting help from anyone else. The things I used to cry about and be afraid of have now become lessons that made me who I am today. I’m happy knowing that I’m doing all of this for myself — no longer relying on my parents, but proving that I can handle life on my own.

My dream is to have a stable career, live a decent lifestyle, and raise a loving family of my own. I don't aspire to be the richest or most famous I just want to live a meaningful and honest life.

Above all, I’m proud because I know I am never truly alone; the Lord is always with me. He is my greatest source of strength, guiding me through every challenge I face.
